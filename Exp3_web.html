About Us:

Sanjay Gandhi National Park, also known as SGNP, is an 87 kmÂ² protected area in Mumbai, Maharashtra. It was established in 1969 with its headquarters situated at Borivali. The 2400-year-old Kanheri caves, sculpted by monks out of the rocky basaltic cliffs, lie within the park.

Come and Experience

Lion/Tiger Safari

Make your visit a memorable one as you watch the big cats at the 'Tiger & Lion Safari' from the safety and comfort of a closed vehicle.
Boating

Just a stone throw away from the main gate is the idyllic artificial water body which allows one to use the pedal boats and go peddling on the scenic, refreshing lake created to facilitate just this experience.
